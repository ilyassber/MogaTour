We MOGALEET believe that each city's history and culture deserve to be well presented and accessible for everyone to perpetuate and share the memories that contribute to our wealth. So because of that our current project is about creating a digital guide as a mobile application that can give a great experience to the visitor in Essaouira city by giving him all the informations about the city and their monuments, history, and culture. also, guided tours that include different sites and activities, selected by category or by preference, plus informations about all the events currently held in Essaouira to make sure that each visitor can get a wonderful visit experience in the city.
